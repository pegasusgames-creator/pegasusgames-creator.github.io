Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026 · Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pegasus Games ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website at pegasusgames-creator.github.io (the "Website") and when you use our mobile applications and games (collectively, the "Services"). We respect your privacy and aim to keep data collection to the minimum needed to provide and improve our Services.

Who we are

Pegasus Games is an independent mobile game studio based in Ukraine. You can reach us any time at pegasusgames@atomicmail.io.

Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Services.

Information you provide directly. If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to share (your email address, the contents of your message, and any attachments).

Information collected automatically through our mobile games. When you install or use our games, certain information may be collected automatically by the operating system or by third-party services we use, including:

  • Device information such as device model, operating system version, language, and mobile advertising identifier (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS).
  • Usage and gameplay data such as levels reached, session length, crash reports, and anonymized performance metrics.
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country or region level), used for ad delivery and analytics.

Information collected through our website. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not set any first-party analytics or advertising cookies. Your web browser's IP address and basic request metadata are processed by GitHub, Inc. in the normal course of serving the page.

How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Display advertising within our games and measure its performance.
  • Diagnose crashes and technical problems.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Third-party services

Our games may use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — to display in-app advertisements. Google may use advertising identifiers and related data to personalize ads where permitted. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Play Services / Google Analytics for Firebase — to deliver the app, collect crash reports, and measure usage.
  • GitHub Pages — to host this website. See GitHub's privacy statement at docs.github.com/site-policy.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's privacy

Our games are designed for general audiences and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it. Where our games are distributed in the Google Play Families program or similar programs, we configure advertising to comply with those programs' requirements.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the European Union, United Kingdom, and Ukraine), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (on Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; on iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising / Tracking).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at pegasusgames@atomicmail.io.

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. Email correspondence is typically retained for up to two years. Anonymized analytics and crash data may be retained longer in aggregated form.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International transfers

Because our third-party providers (Google, GitHub) operate globally, information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. These providers rely on approved transfer mechanisms such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate (for example, via an in-app notice).

Contact us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at pegasusgames@atomicmail.io.
