The Studio

Small team.
Careful craft.

Pegasus Games is an independent mobile game studio based in Ukraine. We make casual puzzle games designed for everyday players — the short commute, the quiet evening, the ten minutes between meetings.

We care about pacing, restraint, and the small details that separate a game you put down after two minutes from one you return to for weeks. Our first releases are in development; you'll find them on the Google Play Store when they're ready.
